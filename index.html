Homesteading

My family and I live in the countryside and lead a homesteading lifestyle. We homeschool our children and actively work towards local food self-sufficiency.

  • We try to grow what we eat (fruits, vegetables, eggs).
  • What we don't [yet] grow, we source from neighboring farms (meat, dairy, extra fruit).
  • What we can't source from neighboring farms, we buy in bulk from local sources (dried beans, wheat grain).
  • What can't be produced locally, we import (coffee, chocolate).
  • We eat fresh produce only when it's in season.
  • We preserve food after harvest to last us through winter.

It's a rewarding lifestyle full of fun challenges.
