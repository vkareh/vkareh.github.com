About

Homesteading

My family and I live in the countryside and lead a homesteading lifestyle. We homeschool our children and actively work towards local food self-sufficiency.

  • We raise a flock of mixed-breed chickens (eggs)
  • We grow a garden every season (vegetables)
  • We tend a few trees and shrubs (fruits)
  • We source food from neighboring farms (meat, dairy, extra produce)
  • We store dry grains grown in our region (beans, wheat)
  • We preserve food after harvest to last us through winter
  • We import a few necessary goodies (coffee, chocolate)
