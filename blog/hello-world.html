Hello World!

November 17, 2008

First post!

I've been a Drupal developer for well over a year now. Up to now, I didn't really give much thought to the fact that I've developed countless modules for it, and modified some others.

I'm a Free Software advocate, so it stands to good behavior that I should release my contributions to the world in the same way that the world provides good code for me. There's a reason why I haven't released any modules yet, and that would be that the modules I create are mostly heavily customized for my clients' applications, so it's not like I can just publish stuff that wouldn't make sense to anyone else but my clients.

Still, that's not good enough of a reason, so I proposed to myself to make every module, where practical, as generic as possible so that the world can benefit from it.

Having said that, it could take me a while to make my modules generic enough for world consumption, but I'll give it my best shot.
