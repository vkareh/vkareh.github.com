DrupalCon 2009: Here I Go!

February 12, 2009

Good fortune is on my side. This was more evident when I found out that the DrupalCon guys were starting a wait-list, starting in order from those that posted a request for a ticket.. The fortunate part was that I was one of the very first in the forum. Phew!

Still, I didn't get my ticket thanks to that wait-list, but because someone that couldn't make it to the conference did the same. I will assume that he went, one by one, FIFO-style, through the list of those who posted in the forum, until he found the next one in line without a ticket. That lucky golden ticket winner was me, and I'm going to Willy Wonka's DrupalCon Factory!

I'm grateful to Cyberswat for selling me his DrupalCon ticket.

On yet another fortunate event, my company not only approved me going to the conference, but they paid the airfare, the hotel, and will pay for all my meals while there.

How cool is that!
